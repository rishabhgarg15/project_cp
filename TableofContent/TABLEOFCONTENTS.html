Course Description

Classical mechanics can be called the first branch of Physics upon which all other branches of Physics have been built. It can describe the motion of macroscopic objects like projectiles, parts in any machinery, spacecraft etc. Thus knowledge of classical mechanics to an engineering student becomes mandatory

Course Outcomes

On completion of this course, the students will have the ability to:

  • Students will attain the basic problem solving skills in problems related to Newtonian mechanics, rotational dynamics, etc.
  • They would be able to apply the concepts of work and energy, linear and angular momentum in their branch of engineering.
  • The concepts related to vector fields, vector calculus, electric field, electrical potential function. potential energy, magnetic field magnetic potential function etc.
  • The four Maxwell’s equations
